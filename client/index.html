We are GateKeeper

GateKeeper is an access management system designed to eliminate the hassels of getting guest badges, in addition to streamlining the process for people who need to come in and out on a regular basis. By taking advantage of something that people have on them at nearly all times, their phones, people never have to worry about losing their IDs. Couple that with the fact that it is easy to manage temporary and permanant access from the server side of things, and you've got an incredibly powerful solution. That's not even mentioning the fact that the whole system can be airgapped if need be for extra security. Sign up today to radically change the way you think about access management!
